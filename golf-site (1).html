Why I Play

I'm Christopher Austin, and golf has been a constant — through every season, every course, every range session that bleeds into dusk. I play out of Hopedale Country Club in central Massachusetts, and I compete whenever the calendar allows.

The 2.9 handicap index, reached in January 2026, is a milestone I'm proud of — but it's also a starting point. The 2026 season has real targets: qualifying for Mass Golf championships and, if the game holds under genuine tournament pressure, a shot at the US Mid-Amateur.

Every round gets logged here — casual and competitive, home and away. The good ones, the bad ones, and all the walks in between.
